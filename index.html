Will I get tax benefits if I donate?

The Shrimad Rajchandra Jivamaitridham Project is funded by Shrimad Rajchandra Jivadaya Trust (SRJT). Every donor who is a resident of India, as well as Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) holding an Indian Passport and also having taxable income in India, can claim income tax benefit from the donation made to SRJT under section 80G of Income Tax Act of 1961.
